# Problem 1

## Problem
Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 15

## AI Solution
AI says: x = 20

## Evaluation
Incorrect

## Error Analysis
- AI misapplied subtraction: should subtract 5 first, then divide by 2

## Correct Solution
2x + 5 = 15  
2x = 15 - 5  
2x = 10  
x = 5

## Key Insight
Always isolate the variable step by step
